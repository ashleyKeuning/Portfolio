Library App

This library app helps High-schoolers from the age between 12 and 15 find the right fitting book to read.

Client: University of applied sciences, Amsterdam
First year
individual Project
January - April 2020

problem statement

High-schoolers between the age of 12 and 15, often have a difficult time to find the right level of book(s) to read, or finding the right book(s) within their favorite genre in the library. 

the goal

The goal of the app is to help children find the right book(s) in the library for them to read. 

how does it work

The app is made for a tablet device, these tablets are found in the libraries. ​

When the children need help with finding the right book(s) to read, they can use the tablet/app for help. the app asks them a couple of questions, like their age, favorite genre etc.  The app provides a list of book recommendations, based on the answers.   They can read the details of the book, which help them find the book in the library and they can save the book(s) in their  wishlist. ​

the design proces

research of the corporate identity

first interface ideas

The actions that are performed on tablets in the library. It must be quick and clear, otherwise the children will drop out, in addition, it must be very appealing to them to use the tablet. To design this by, for example, using large letters and bright but also dark colors to compensate for the bright colors.
